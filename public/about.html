Our Story

Where Passion Meets Flavor

Welcome to DineFine

At DineFine, we believe that every meal tells a story. From the tantalizing aroma of freshly prepared spices to the harmonious blend of flavors on every plate, we strive to create a dining experience that delights your senses and warms your soul.

Founded with a passion for culinary excellence, DineFine has become a destination for food lovers seeking authentic flavors and exceptional service. Located at the heart of Al Ain, Abu Dhabi, our restaurant brings together the best of international cuisine.

Our mission is simple: to bring people together over great food. We pride ourselves on offering a warm, inviting ambiance and service that makes every guest feel like family.

Meet Our Head Chef

Chef Mohsin Ahmad

With over 15 years of culinary expertise spanning prestigious kitchens across the Middle East and Europe, Chef Mohsin brings a wealth of knowledge and creativity to DineFine.

Chef Mohsin's philosophy is simple: respect the ingredients, honor the traditions, and never stop innovating. His signature dishes blend authentic Arabic flavors with modern techniques.

Specialties:

  • Traditional Arabic Mandi & Kabsa
  • Charcoal Grilled Premium Steaks
  • Fusion Mediterranean Cuisine
  • Signature Spice Blends
